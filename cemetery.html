Tour of the Cemetery

About Us

Welcome to the Freemium Graveyard, a place where we exhume the truth about "free" services. We're dedicated to dissecting the fine print, exposing hidden fees, and helping you navigate the murky waters of freemium offerings. Our goal is to empower you to make informed decisions and avoid the pitfalls of predatory pricing and deceptive marketing tactics. We believe that everyone deserves access to honest and transparent information about the services they use, and we're here to shine a light on the dark side of "free."

Meet Your Gravedigger

Hi, I'm Hanna, a 21-year-old jack-of-all-trades with a passion for writing. I'm also perpetually broke, which is why I'm so interested in helping others find genuinely free or affordable services. I believe that everyone should have access to quality resources, regardless of their budget. My mission is to dig deep into the world of freemium and share my findings with you. Fun fact: I'm a huge $uicideboy$ fan!
